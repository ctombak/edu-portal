Integrate the day's learning into a concrete, personal action plan that maps directly to participants' current product initiatives.

## Product Lifecycle Action Planning

The Product Lifecycle Action Plan maps to your current reality: Current Phase (where is your product in the lifecycle right now?), Biggest Gap (which framework or practice from today would have the highest impact?), Quick Win (one thing you can implement in the next 48 hours), Process Improvement (one workflow or governance change to propose this month), Team Capability (one skill or practice to develop in your team this quarter), Risk to Address (one risk you've been ignoring that needs a mitigation plan), Accountability Partner (a colleague from today who will check in at 30 days).

**Framework: Product Lifecycle Action Plan** — Current Phase, Biggest Gap, Quick Win (48 hours), Process Improvement (this month), Team Capability (this quarter), Risk to Address, Accountability Partner.

## Key Takeaway Sharing & Commitment

Participants share their top insight and first action with the group. The session closes with a reflection on the full lifecycle journey covered during the day and a commitment to applying the frameworks in their current product initiatives.
