## Product Lifecycle Action Planning

- The **Product Lifecycle Action Plan** maps to your current reality:
  - **Current Phase** -- where is your product in the lifecycle right now?
  - **Biggest Gap** -- which framework or practice from today would have the highest impact?
  - **Quick Win** -- one thing you can implement in the next 48 hours
  - **Process Improvement** -- one workflow or governance change to propose this month
  - **Team Capability** -- one skill or practice to develop in your team this quarter
  - **Risk to Address** -- one risk you've been ignoring that needs a mitigation plan
  - **Accountability Partner** -- a colleague from today who will check in at 30 days

**Framework: Product Lifecycle Action Plan** -- Current Phase, Biggest Gap, Quick Win (48 hours), Process Improvement (this month), Team Capability (this quarter), Risk to Address, Accountability Partner.

<!-- notes: Give participants 10-15 minutes to fill out their action plan individually. Then pair them up with an accountability partner. The 30-day check-in dramatically increases follow-through. -->

## Key Takeaway Sharing & Commitment

- Participants share their top insight and first action with the group
- Reflect on the full lifecycle journey covered during the day:
  - Discovery -- knowing what you need
  - Evaluation -- choosing the right partners and architectures
  - Planning -- governing effectively
  - Build -- executing with quality
  - Launch -- deploying safely
  - Operate -- improving continuously
- Commit to applying the frameworks in current product initiatives
- The lifecycle is a living practice, not a one-time checklist

> The frameworks you've learned today are only valuable if you use them. Start with one. Start tomorrow.
