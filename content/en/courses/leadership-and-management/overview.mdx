This program bridges traditional hierarchical management with the adaptive, influence-based leadership required in today's organizations. Drawing on frameworks from Harvard Business School, McKinsey, Center for Creative Leadership, Ronald Heifetz's adaptive leadership research, and Maister/Green/Galford's trust framework, participants gain practical tools they can apply immediately.

The training brings a global multinational perspective while addressing the unique dynamics of the Turkish business context—high power distance, collective loyalty, and cyclical economic pressures.

## Facilitator

Cihat Tombak brings over 15 years of experience in software development leadership at global multinational companies. His engineering management background provides practical, rigorous frameworks for institutional leaders seeking to modernize their approach while respecting local dynamics.
