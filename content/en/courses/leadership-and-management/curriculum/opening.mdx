## The 2026 Leadership Landscape

71% of leaders report increased stress, 40% have considered stepping back from leadership, and 75% report burnout symptoms. Organizations are flattening—fewer layers, wider spans. Leaders' influence extends 3x further than a decade ago. Positional authority matters less while relational capital matters more. AI is reshaping decision-making while human connection becomes more critical.

> In our culture, the leader is often seen as the 'parent' who has all the answers. Today, we look at why the best global leaders don't have all the answers—they have the best questions.

## Leadership Crucibles

Participants share formative leadership experiences—the failures, conflicts, crises, and situations that defined their leadership journey. These crucible moments reveal that leadership isn't learned in classrooms—it's forged in difficulty. The session surfaces collective themes about what leadership truly requires.
