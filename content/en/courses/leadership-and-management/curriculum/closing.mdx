Integrate the day's learning into a concrete, personal action plan with accountability structures for follow-through.

## Personal Action Planning

The Leadership Action Plan covers: Key Insight (the most important learning from today), I Will Start (one new practice this week), I Will Stop (one habit that isn't serving me), I Will Continue (one strength to leverage more intentionally), I Will Delegate (one thing to hand off—and to whom), My First Action (specific thing in the next 48 hours), My Accountability Partner (who will hold me accountable), and My 30-Day Check-In (when and how to review progress).

**Framework: 100-Day Action Plan** — Start/Stop/Continue framework with specific first action, delegation commitment, accountability partner, and 30-day check-in.

## Key Takeaway Sharing & Commitment

Participants share their top insight and first action with the group. The session closes with a reflection on the day's journey and a commitment to applying the frameworks in practice.
